About us

Welcome to Cafe Fuencarral. We are excited to bring you the wonderful flavours of Spanish tapas into the heart of the Bristol city center.

Tapas is about sharing delicious food with close friends and family, making it the perfect choice for any social occasion. Come take a look for yourself at our cafe where you can experience the fantastic taste combined with vibrant colours of tapas. We look forward to seeing you and sharing our passion for Spanish tapas.
